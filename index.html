A personal reset

The 3-Day Unstuck Challenge

Three days. Three angles. One honest look at where you are and where you want to go.

Day One
Name What Hurts
You can't fix what you won't face. Today is about honest acknowledgment — no fixing, no planning. Just truth.
Why this comes first

Feeling stuck isn't a character flaw — it's a signal. Something in you knows a gap exists between where you are and where you want to be. Today we listen to that signal instead of muffling it.

The 5 Whys
Pick the biggest thing bothering you. Ask "why?" five times to get beneath the surface complaint to the real issue.
Why am I feeling stuck? (Why #1) But why is that? (Why #2) And why does that matter? (Why #3) What does that tell me? (Why #4) What's the core truth? (Why #5)
Day Two
Find What's Missing
Now that you've named the pain, we go looking for the gap — the distance between who you are now and who you actually want to be.
The reframe

Feeling stuck usually isn't about lacking willpower. It's about a misalignment — between your values and your daily life, between your potential and your environment, or between who you are and who others expect you to be.

Day Three
Choose One Move
Clarity without action is just daydreaming. Today you commit to one small, concrete thing that begins to close the gap.
The secret about getting unstuck

You don't need a life overhaul. You need one honest decision and one small action. Momentum builds from movement, not from having it all figured out. A single honest step beats a perfect plan that never starts.
